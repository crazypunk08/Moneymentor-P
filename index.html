About the product

Money Mentor, powered by Google's Gemini API, is a sophisticated financial assistant designed to help users navigate the complexities of the financial world with ease. By offering real-time data, personalized advice, and interactive financial planning tools, Money Mentor empowers users to make smarter financial decisions and achieve their financial goals.

Key features

Individual Investors:

Tailored advice for personal investment strategies.Tool for financial advisors to enhance client interactions.

Businesses:

Assists small and medium-sized businesses with financial management, cash flow analysis, and investment planning.

Acts as an educational resource for users looking to improve their financial literacy and understanding of market dynamics.
